Employee, Payroll calculation, employee registration and cancellation


- Wage calculation for workers, directors, founders and owners - net, gross, taxes and contributions, net wage disbursements through the implementation of administrative and / or court injunctions;
- Calculation of all salary compensations (sick leave for up to 30 days, over 30 days, maternity leave, etc.), transfer of these calculations to the social;
- Calculation of all other personal earnings of employees (recourse, warm meal, travel expenses);
- Calculation of benefits under labor contracts, temporary and occasional affairs, and the like;
- Calculation of rent, capital gains;
- Payment of salaries and other taxable payments of analytical wage bookkeeping;
- Keeping records of all settlements and payments, and in the preparation of M-4 and PPP forms
- In all settlements, it is assumed that appropriate tax returns are submitted and submitted to the Tax Administration.


In service personnel - it is socially worth the postulate that the Agency organizes and completes the whole job:
- Preparation of employment contracts for the employment of workers, contracts on temporary and occasional affairs, contracts for supplementary work, work contracts and the like;
- Registrations and dismissals of employees, directors, founders and owners, in the Pension and Disability Insurance Fund - Forms M-1 and M-1 / SP;
- Registrations and dismissals of employees, directors, founders and owners, in the Health Fund - Forms M and M-1;
- Making a decision on annual leave, employee leave and similar;
- Creation of various employment certificates, amount of income and the like;
- Registration of salaries for entrepreneurs in the analytical records of the Pension and Disability Insurance Fund;
- Enrollment of employees in companies - preparation of M-4 form;
- Creating new and checking existing health booklets.
